📖 About Kimongore Junior School

🏅 Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2008, Kimongore Junior School is a private, co-educational day and boarding primary school located in the heart of Isingiro District, Western Uganda. We pride ourselves on delivering affordable, high-quality education that combines modern pedagogy, a well-equipped computer laboratory, and strong moral values.

🎯 Vision: To be a center of academic excellence and moral integrity in Isingiro and beyond.

🌟 Mission: To nurture confident, God-fearing, and responsible citizens through holistic education, modern infrastructure (including a full ICT lab), and dedicated staff.
